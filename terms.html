1. What StepPact Is

StepPact is a fitness accountability platform. Users join step challenges with a defined daily step goal and duration. Some challenges are free; others require a Platform Access Fee to enter and offer rewards to participants who complete the challenge.

StepPact is a skill-based competition platform, not gambling. Challenge outcomes are determined entirely by each participant's own sustained physical effort, discipline, and consistency in meeting daily step goals. There is no element of chance, no random drawings, no odds, and no wagering on outcomes outside your own control. Whether you earn a reward depends solely on whether you walk.

4. Challenges and Completion Rules

Each challenge specifies a daily step goal, a start date, and a duration in days. Specific rules are displayed in the App on each challenge's page before you join.

  • A day is completed when your verified step count for that calendar day meets or exceeds the challenge's daily goal.
  • To qualify for a reward in a paid challenge, you must complete the required number of days specified by the challenge rules. Unless stated otherwise on the challenge page, one rest day per challenge is permitted (you qualify by completing at least duration minus one days).
  • Step data is measured by your device's motion sensors and/or Apple HealthKit. You are responsible for carrying your device and granting the required permissions; days without recorded data count as not completed.

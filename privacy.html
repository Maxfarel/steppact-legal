StepPact Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

StepPact ("StepPact," "we," "us," or "our") is a fitness accountability platform operated by Maksim Zhytkevich (sole proprietor), Greenville, South Carolina, USA.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through the StepPact mobile application (the "App"), how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

Contact for all privacy matters: supportsteppact@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

Account information. Your name, email address, and username. Authentication is handled by our identity provider, Clerk.

Health and activity data. With your explicit permission, the App reads your step count from your device's motion sensors and/or Apple HealthKit. We store daily step totals, hourly breakdowns and source-attribution metadata used for fraud prevention and audit, and challenge completion records (which days you met your goal). We do not collect other health metrics.

Payment and transaction information. Payments and payouts are processed by Stripe. We never receive or store your full card or bank-account numbers. We store your in-app transaction history (deposits, Platform Access Fees, prize credits, payouts) and your internal wallet balance.

Tax information. Where required by U.S. law, we may collect tax identification information (e.g., a W-9) from users who approach or exceed reporting thresholds for prize winnings.

Identity verification. Before processing certain payouts, we (through Stripe) may require identity verification information to comply with legal and anti-fraud requirements.

Fraud-prevention data. To prevent multiple-account abuse and payment fraud, we collect and process device signals (device model, operating system version, app version, IP address) and a one-way hashed fingerprint of your payment method provided by Stripe. We cannot reconstruct your full payment details from this hash. We also log fraud signals evaluated and their outcomes.

Notification tokens. If you enable push notifications, we collect your device push token and notification preferences to send transactional and service-related messages.

Location information. Paid challenges are unavailable in certain U.S. states (see our Terms of Service). To enforce these rules, the App may verify your approximate location when you join a paid challenge. We do not track your location continuously or store a history of your movements.

Device and usage data. Basic technical data such as device model, OS version, app version, language, and diagnostic logs, used to operate and improve the App.

2. How We Use Your Health Data (HealthKit)

  • We only read step and activity data you authorize; we never write any data to HealthKit.
  • Health data is used solely to track progress in challenges you join, verify completion, display your statistics, determine results, and detect fraud.
  • Health data is never used for advertising, marketing, profiling, or data mining.
  • Health data is never sold or disclosed to advertisers or data brokers; it is shared only with our infrastructure providers (Section 5) strictly as necessary to run the App.
  • We rely on the aggregated step counts reported by your device and cannot independently verify their accuracy; counts may vary between devices and tracking methods.
  • You can revoke access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → StepPact. Revoking access will prevent you from participating in challenges that require step tracking.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate challenges: track progress, determine qualifying participants, calculate and credit prizes
  • Process payments, maintain your wallet, and execute payouts
  • Display leaderboards and activity feeds (your name/username, avatar, step totals, and challenge completions may be visible to other users)
  • Verify identity and comply with tax-reporting obligations
  • Enforce eligibility rules, our Terms of Service, and anti-fraud measures
  • Send transactional and service-related notifications
  • Provide support and respond to your requests

We do not sell your personal information and do not display third-party advertising.

4. Tax Reporting

Prize winnings may be taxable. For U.S. users, we may be required to report winnings of $600 or more per calendar year to the Internal Revenue Service and to collect the tax information needed to do so. You are responsible for any taxes owed on prizes you receive.

5. Sharing Your Information

We share information only with:

  • Service providers that power the App: Clerk (authentication), Convex (backend and database hosting), Stripe (payment processing, payouts, identity verification, fraud signals), and Expo (app delivery and push notifications). Each processes data only as needed to perform its service.
  • Other users, limited to social features: your name, username, avatar, step totals, and challenge completion events appear in leaderboards and activity feeds.
  • Legal authorities, if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of StepPact or others.
  • In a business transfer: if StepPact is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred; we will provide notice before it becomes subject to a different policy.

6. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above and to meet legal obligations:

  • Account information: while your account is active, and up to 3 years after closure for dispute resolution and legal compliance.
  • Health and activity data: while active, and up to 3 years after closure for dispute resolution, fraud prevention, and historical challenge verification. You may request earlier deletion, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Financial and transaction records: at least 7 years to comply with tax and financial-record laws.
  • Fraud-prevention records (device signals, hashed payment fingerprints): up to 7 years to detect repeat abuse.
  • Notification tokens: deleted on account closure or when you revoke push permissions.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time in the App (Profile → Danger Zone → Delete Account). Upon deletion, your personal information is removed or irreversibly anonymized, except records we must retain under Section 6 (financial, tax, and fraud-prevention).

8. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest with reputable cloud providers, protected by access controls and authentication. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take commercially reasonable measures to protect your information. If a breach affecting your personal data occurs, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.

9. Children

The App is for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you may request to know what personal information we collect and how we use it, request deletion (subject to the legal retention exceptions in Section 6), and you have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising these rights. We do not sell personal information. To make a request, email supportsteppact@gmail.com with "Privacy Request" in the subject line.

11. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. You can do most of this directly in the App or by emailing supportsteppact@gmail.com. We respond to verified requests within the timeframes required by applicable law.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. The updated version will be posted in the App with a new effective date; material changes will be communicated in the App.

13. Contact

Questions or requests: supportsteppact@gmail.com
